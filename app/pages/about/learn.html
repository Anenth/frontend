How It Works

Bountysource is the marketplace for
spending money toward
and
earning money from
open-source development.
We have two mechanisms to do so: fundraisers and bounties.

Fundraisers

Great ideas often need money to succeed.
Have a project that needs funding? Fundraisers are your best bet.

Fundraisers are campaigns that allow developers to raise money for their next big project by setting a funding goal and offering up rewards.

The possibilities are endless! You can raise money for:

  • An entirely new project
  • A big update to an existing project
  • Hiring developers to join your team
  • Placing bounties on issues within your own project
  • Quitting your job to spend all your time on your project
  • and more!

From idea to reality in 3 easy steps!

  1. Create Fundraiser
  2. Backers pledge to Fundraiser
  3. Project is funded - ideas become reality!

Get started!

  • Go create a fundraiser now
  • Find projects that need your support

Bounties

Need a bug fixed? Want to see new features?
Bounties are monetary rewards placed on existing issues within open-source projects.

Users of open-source - individuals and organizations alike - can use bounties to incentivize developers to address issues most important to them.

Developers can earn bounties by addressing issues your community wants resolved. At the same time, you are contributing to open-source software that will benefit everyone.

  1. Search for an issue
  2. Place a bounty and get others to do the same
  3. Developer submits a solution
  4. Solution is accepted
  5. Issue is resolved - everyone is happy!

Get started!

  • Find bounties to solve
  • Search for issues within your favorite projects
